Logistic Regression Analysis for Credit Card Risk Assessment

The Logistic Regression Analysis for Credit Card Risk Assessment is a powerful project developed to aid credit card companies in evaluating and managing potential risks associated with their customers. Leveraging the robust capabilities of logistic regression, this Python-based solution empowers financial institutions to make informed decisions by quantifying the probability of credit defaults and identifying high-risk individuals. Using a comprehensive dataset of customer attributes and credit histories, this project applies logistic regression, a widely-used statistical technique, to model the relationship between various factors and the likelihood of credit card default. By analyzing key variables such as income, age, credit score, and transaction history, the model provides a reliable risk assessment framework, enabling credit card companies to identify individuals with a higher probability of defaulting on payments.
